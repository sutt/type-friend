Welcome to the Mines

You have entered the forbidden depths

Congratulations, brave adventurer! You have successfully cast the ancient spell and gained entry to the legendary mines. The air grows thick with mystery and the scent of untold treasures.

Before you stretches a vast network of tunnels carved deep into the mountain's heart. Golden veins run through the walls like rivers of light, and the echo of your footsteps mingles with the distant rumble of something ancient stirring in the depths.

Legend speaks of a great dragon who guards the most precious treasures. Will you dare to venture deeper into these hallowed halls, or will you turn back while you still can?

Beware: Those who enter the mines rarely return unchanged. The treasures within are matched only by the perils that guard them.
